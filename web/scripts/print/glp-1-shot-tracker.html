Missed a dose? Each medication's label gives a catch-up window (Wegovy: 48 h before next dose · Ozempic: 5 days · Zepbound/Mounjaro: 4 days). Never double up — when unsure, call your pharmacist.

Weigh weekly, judge monthly. Same scale, same conditions, once a week. Day-to-day swings are water, not fat — the trend over 4+ weeks is the real signal.

Rough week after a dose increase? Small, low-fat, bland meals; stay upright after eating; sip fluids all day. Severe abdominal pain or vomiting that won't stop → call your prescriber.

Bring this sheet to appointments. Eight weeks of doses, weights, and side-effect notes is exactly the record titration decisions are made on.
