# Pricing

Source pricing matrix for Glu Pro.
Pricing is organized into two market tiers:
- `1` = premium markets
- `2` = value markets

Monthly prices are fixed by tier:
- Tier `1`: `$9.99`
- Tier `2`: `$4.99`

Yearly prices are fixed by tier:
- Tier `1`: `$79.99`
- Tier `2`: `$39.99`

Netflix has no usable monthly price in this table for China mainland, Kosovo, and Russia.
All countries remain Pro markets.
